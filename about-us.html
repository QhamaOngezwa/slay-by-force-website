About Us

SlayBy4orCe is a home-based hair boutique in Johannesburg central that specialises in a wide range of braid styles. From cornrows and Bantu knots to Faux locs and more. Any hairtsyle of your choice. We got you! We offer both in-studio appointments and convenient house calls. So whether you prefer to visit us or get pampered in the comfort of your own home, we got you covered! Let us bring the hairstyle of your dreams right to your doorstep!

Our Mission

SlayBy4orCe was created to make women feel good about themselves without breaking the bank. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident, radiant, and unstoppable. And nothing boosts confidence like a fresh, new hairdo. At SlayBy4orCe, we cater to those queens who want to look good at an affordable price!
